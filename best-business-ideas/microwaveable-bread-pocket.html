Invent a microwaveable bread-pocket food brand. You know, the kind filled with cheese and other fillings. Invest a lot at first in quality ingredients to help promote the product. However, your strategy over time is to reduce these costs. The trick is you design the microwaveable bread-pocket so that the cheese is always at the right temperature to burn the eaters tongue, scalding as many taste buds as possible. This is what you can leverage to gradually decrease the ingredient quality without anyone noticing. Profit margins should begin to expand quickly if you do this right.
